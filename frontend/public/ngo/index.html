Our Story

Who We Are

Shri Avadh Bihari Charitable Trust was established with a single guiding principle — selfless service. We believe that even the smallest act of kindness can create a ripple effect of transformation in our communities.

Guided by compassion and social responsibility, we work to ensure that every individual — regardless of background — has access to fundamental necessities, personal dignity, and pathways for growth.

🌱

Our Vision

A society where no one is left behind — where every person lives with dignity.

🤝

Our Mission

Deliver impactful initiatives through healthcare, education, and community empowerment.

💛

Our Values

Seva (selfless service), compassion, transparency, and respect for every person we serve.

What We Do

Our Programs

Five core initiatives, each designed to address a fundamental need in the communities we serve.

📚

Education Support

School kits, books, uniforms, and financial aid to children from low-income families — removing barriers to learning.

🏥

Health & Wellness Camps

Free medical evaluations, eye testing, medicine distribution, and health awareness in underserved rural communities.

🍱

Food & Essentials

Regular food and clothing drives serving homeless individuals, widows, and economically disadvantaged households.

👴

Elderly & Widows Support

Delivering necessities and emotional support to senior citizens and widows — ensuring dignity in their golden years.

🌾

Rural Development

Improving rural living standards through sanitation promotion, skills training, and income-generating opportunities.
