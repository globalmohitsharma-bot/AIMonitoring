About the Trust

Established on the principles of Seva, compassion, and service — our story is one of ordinary people doing extraordinary things.

Who We Are

Our Foundation

Shri Avadh Bihari Charitable Trust is a registered non-profit organization based in Jwalapur, Haridwar, Uttarakhand — dedicated to serving humanity through selfless action.

Founded with the guiding principle of Seva — selfless service — the Trust was established to address the gaps in healthcare, education, nutrition, and social support that affect vulnerable communities across our region.

We operate through a network of dedicated volunteers, medical professionals, educators, and community leaders who believe that dignity is a right, not a privilege. Every camp we organize, every meal we distribute, every child we support — each act is done with love and without expectation.

The Trust is registered and operates with full transparency. Our address is Shop No. 2, Ucchi Sadak, Arya Nagar, Jwalapur, Haridwar, Uttarakhand – 249407.

🌟 Our Vision

A society where every individual — regardless of background — lives with dignity, health, and opportunity. Where no child misses school for lack of supplies. Where no elder is forgotten.

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver impactful, community-driven programs in healthcare, education, food security, and rural empowerment — bringing sustainable change to lives across Uttarakhand.

💛 Seva First

Every action we take is rooted in selflessness. No hierarchy, no bureaucracy — just volunteers showing up and serving with their whole heart.

🔍 Full Transparency

We operate with complete openness. Every rupee donated is accounted for. Every activity is documented and shared with our community of supporters.

A Milestone Moment

Inauguration of Our Office

The inauguration of the Shri Avadh Bihari Charitable Trust office marked a defining chapter in our journey — formalizing our commitment to serving the community.

The office inauguration ceremony brought together trustees, volunteers, community members, and well-wishers who have supported our mission from the very beginning. It was a moment of celebration, reflection, and renewed commitment to the people we serve.

The Trust's new office now serves as the central hub for all our activities — from coordinating camp registrations and volunteer management to processing donations and planning outreach programs.

The event was covered by local press and marked an important step in our growth from a community volunteer group to a formally established charitable organization with a permanent base in Haridwar.

Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

From a small group of volunteers to a formally registered Trust — here are the key moments that have shaped who we are.

🌱
Foundation
The Trust is Born
Shri Avadh Bihari Charitable Trust is founded in Haridwar with a small but dedicated team of volunteers, driven by the belief that community service is the highest form of devotion.
🏥
First Health Camp
Free Medical Camp Organized
The Trust organizes its first free medical camp, providing check-ups, medicines, and health consultations to over 100 villagers who had never before received medical attention.
📚
Education Drive
School Kit Distribution Begins
Launched the Education Support Program, distributing school bags, books, and uniforms to children from low-income families — ensuring no child misses school for lack of supplies.
👁️
Eye Care Initiative
Free Eye Testing Camps
Partnering with ophthalmologists, the Trust begins organizing dedicated eye testing camps — providing free eye exams and prescription glasses to hundreds of villagers with undiagnosed vision problems.
🏛️
Office Inauguration
Official Office Established
The Trust inaugurates its permanent office at Shop No. 2, Ucchi Sadak, Arya Nagar, Jwalapur, Haridwar — formalizing operations and creating a permanent hub for all activities.
🌟
Growing Impact
500+ Lives Impacted
With five active programs, 20+ camps organized, and a growing volunteer network, the Trust continues expanding its reach across Haridwar district and neighboring areas.
