Our Foundation

Built on Compassion,
Driven by Purpose

Shri Avadh Bihari Charitable Trust was founded on a single belief — that dignity is a right, not a privilege. Based in Haridwar, Uttarakhand, we organize free healthcare camps, educational drives, and community welfare programs across the region.

Every rupee donated, every volunteer hour given, goes directly to the people we serve. No overhead, no administration costs taken from donations — just pure Seva.

Selfless Service — Seva is our guiding principle
Full Transparency — Every activity is accountable
Community First — Decisions made by those we serve
