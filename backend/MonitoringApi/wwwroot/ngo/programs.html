Program 1

Education Support

No child should miss out on education because their family cannot afford school supplies. We distribute school kits, books, uniforms, and stationery to children from low-income families across Haridwar.

  • Comprehensive school kit: bag, books, stationery, uniform
  • Targeted support for girls’ education and first-generation learners
  • Supplementary tuition and homework support sessions
  • Scholarship guidance for deserving students
  • Awareness campaigns to reduce school dropout rates

Our Education Support Program ensures that every child has what they need to attend school with confidence and dignity. Because education is not a privilege — it’s a right.

Program 2

Health & Wellness Camps

Preventable illness remains a leading cause of hardship in rural communities. Our free medical and eye camps bring qualified doctors and specialists directly to villages, removing every barrier to care.

  • Free medical check-ups, diagnostics, and consultations
  • Specialist eye testing camps with free prescription glasses
  • Medicines and basic health supplies at no cost
  • Blood pressure, diabetes, and chronic illness screening
  • Women’s health and maternal care awareness sessions
  • Dental check-up and hygiene awareness drives

We partner with medical professionals and hospitals to bring comprehensive healthcare to those who have never seen a doctor. Health is wealth — and we make sure everyone can access it.

Program 3

Food & Essential Supplies

Hunger should never be the reason a family cannot thrive. We organise regular food distribution drives and provide essential household supplies to families facing economic hardship across Haridwar.

  • Monthly ration kits: rice, dal, oil, sugar, salt, and flour
  • Ready cooked meals at special community events and festivals
  • Essential hygiene supplies: soap, toothpaste, sanitary products
  • Blankets and warm clothing during winter months
  • Emergency food relief during floods, illness, or calamity

No matter one’s caste, religion, or background — when someone comes to us hungry, we feed them. That is our most fundamental promise.

Program 4

Elderly & Widow Welfare

Our elders deserve to age with dignity, care, and community. We provide dedicated support for senior citizens and widows who are isolated, abandoned, or unable to meet their basic needs.

  • Regular welfare visits and emotional support for isolated elders
  • Essential supplies and medicines for elderly widows
  • Support navigating government welfare schemes and pensions
  • Community gatherings to reduce loneliness and isolation
  • Dedicated health camps for senior citizens

In a society that often forgets its oldest members, we choose to show up — to listen, to serve, and to remind them that they are valued, seen, and loved.

Program 5

Rural Empowerment

Lasting change requires investment in communities, not just individuals. Our rural development program builds sustainable livelihoods, improves village infrastructure, and empowers rural women to become financially independent.

  • Skill workshops: tailoring, handicrafts, digital literacy
  • Self-Help Group (SHG) formation and mentoring for women
  • Awareness on government schemes, rights, and entitlements
  • Basic sanitation and hygiene infrastructure support
  • Tree plantation drives and environmental awareness camps

We believe that empowering a village means empowering every person in it. When rural communities thrive, India thrives.
